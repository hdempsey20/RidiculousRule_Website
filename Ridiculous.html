Most Ridiculous Rule

One ridiculous rule I have heard is that some teachers will choose what school supplies they want you to use for their class. I think you should be able to use what works best for you. Also, I feel that we are old enough to be able to decide what we want to use.

My Experience

Some of my teachers this year have required me to use certain school supplies. For example, my teacher would like us to use a binder instead of a notebook in chemistry.
